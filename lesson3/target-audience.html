The target audience are the gentle folk of Roygbiv city. The people of the town are friendly and inclusive. The target audience is a lot younger, people in their 40s are probably the oldest people going to it.

    Five Scenarios

  1. What are some fun events happening in Roygbiv in the next month?
  2. What is the weather for the next 5 days in Roygbiv City?
  3. What local restaurants are there in Roygbiv City?
  4. Who should I contact to book a booth for a festival?
  5. What's the weather going to be like on the day of an event?

    Two personas:

  1. Daniel is a food truck owner that frequently goes to Roygbiv for catering. He needs to know what the weather will be like on the day he's booked for an event.
  2. Lillian loves to go on outdoor dates in town like for picnics and hikes.The weather is known to be temperamental so she needs to know what nights will have the best weather for her dates.
